Security & Privacy

All customer data is encrypted in transit using TLS 1.2 or higher.
Sensitive information is never logged or stored in plain text.

Uploaded documents are used exclusively for retrieval purposes and are not
used to train machine learning models.

Access to internal systems is restricted based on role-based access control (RBAC).
Regular security audits are conducted to ensure compliance with internal policies.

For security-related inquiries, customers should contact support directly.
